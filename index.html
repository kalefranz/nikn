My name is Kale Franz. NotInKansasnow is my personal blog about some things I like: coding, semiconductors, light, my dog. Yeah, random. Such it is for a personal blog. Who am I? Currently a software engineer for 23andMe. I have a PhD in that last part, but software is just too much fun!
